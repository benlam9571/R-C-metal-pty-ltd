About R&C Metal

R&C Metal Pty Ltd provides Australia-based project coordination, customer communication, quote support, and supply access, backed by manufacturing partners with 15 extrusion lines, 80,000 t annual capacity, and 10+ international certifications.

Annual Capacity 80K Tons Per Year

Australia-Based Project Support

R&C Metal Pty Ltd is based in Australia and supports local customers with communication, requirement clarification, quote coordination, project documentation, and supply follow-up.

Manufacturing capability is provided by partners in Longkou, Shandong, China, operating 15 extrusion lines from 2,000 t to 10,000 t press force and producing over 80,000 tonnes of precision aluminium profiles per year — up to 950 mm × 580 mm cross-section and 30 metres in length.

The supply range covers six product lines: aluminium structures, high-end materials, rail transit processing, commercial vehicles, building formwork, and automotive parts, supported by the manufacturing partner's quality and certification systems.

Manufacturing Capabilities

15 Extrusion Production Lines

Manufacturing partners operate 15 extrusion presses from 2,000 t to 10,000 t press force, enabling production of both fine decorative profiles and heavy structural sections.

80K Tons Per Year Annual Capacity

The partner's 80,000 t annual capacity supports large infrastructure and industrial supply programs, while R&C Metal helps Australian clients coordinate timing and communication.

950mm Maximum Profile Width

Manufacturing partners can produce aluminium extrusion profiles up to 950 mm × 580 mm in cross-section, supporting wide structural sections and industrial custom profiles.

30m Maximum Profile Length

Extruded profiles up to 30 metres in length reduce joints in structural applications and suit bridge decks, dome ribs, rail car sections, and other large components.

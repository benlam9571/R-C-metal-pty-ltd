Our Six Product Lines

R&C Metal provides Australia-based project support, customer communication, and quote coordination across six aluminium product lines backed by certified manufacturing capability: structures, high-end materials, rail transit, commercial vehicles, building formwork, and automotive parts.

Aluminum Structure Products

A comprehensive range of prefabricated aluminium structures — pedestrian footbridges (L/B/SL/R systems), spatial domes, helicopter helipads, corridor bridges, and observation towers. All bolt-connected and factory-assembled. One-third to one-half the weight of steel, with a 30–50+ year service life.

  • Product Types: Footbridges · Domes · Helipads · Corridor Bridges · Towers
  • Weight vs Steel: 1/3 to 1/2 (structures); 30% of concrete (helipads)
  • Service Life: 30–50+ years depending on product type
  • Assembly: Fully bolt-connected, no site welding
  • Recyclability: ≥95% recoverable at end of life

High-End Aluminum Materials

Premium aluminium alloy billets, slabs, and custom extrusion profiles — spanning 6xxx and 7xxx series alloys. Backed by 15 extrusion presses from 2,000 to 10,000 tonnes, producing sections up to 950 mm × 580 mm and lengths up to 30 m. In-house anodising, powder coating, and electrophoresis finishing for full surface protection.

  • Extrusion Lines: 15 presses, 2,000 t – 10,000 t
  • Profile Size: Up to 950 mm × 580 mm
  • Length: Up to 30 m per piece
  • Alloys: 6xxx and 7xxx series for structural and industrial use
  • Surface Treatment: In-house anodising, powder coating, electrophoresis

Rail Transit Deep Processing

Certified aluminium extrusion profiles and deep-processed components for metro, high-speed rail, light rail, and tram systems. Fully compliant with IRIS and EN15085-2 CL1 welding standards — covering carbody panels, floor structures, roof sections, and interior framing.

  • Standards: IRIS · EN15085-2 CL1
  • Profile & Length: Up to 950 mm × 580 mm, lengths to 30 m
  • Applications: Metro carbodies, HSR floor panels, light rail framing
  • Processes: Extrusion, CNC machining, TIG/MIG welding, surface treatment
  • Quality: 100% dimensional and mechanical testing per batch

Aluminum Alloy Commercial Vehicles

Structural aluminium components and body systems for box-body trucks, wing-open semi-trailers, and refrigerated transport vehicles. Replacing steel with aluminium reduces vehicle tare weight by 30–40%, directly increasing net payload and improving fleet fuel economy.

  • Applications: Box trucks, wing-open trailers, refrigerated vans
  • Weight Saving: 30–40% vs. equivalent steel construction
  • Payload Gain: Measurable increase in net cargo capacity per vehicle
  • Components: Custom extruded floor beams, side panels, corner posts
  • Finish: Anodised or powder-coated for corrosion and UV resistance
Box Body Refrigerated Wing-Open 30–40% Lighter Payload Optimised

Aluminum Alloy Building Formwork

Lightweight, reusable aluminium alloy formwork systems for concrete casting in residential and commercial construction. With a typical reuse count exceeding 300 cycles, aluminium formwork dramatically reduces construction waste and labour costs vs. timber or steel alternatives — delivering a superior smooth concrete surface with no plastering required.

  • Reuse Cycles: 300+ per panel set
  • Weight: ~18–22 kg/m² (vs. 45–55 kg/m² for steel formwork)
  • Application: Slabs, walls, columns, beams, stairwells
  • Surface Finish: Smooth concrete, no plastering required
  • Assembly: Pin-and-wedge or bolt system, no crane required

Aluminum Automotive Parts

Precision aluminium alloy parts for passenger vehicles and new energy vehicles (NEV), manufactured under the TS16949 automotive quality management system. Products include structural body parts, chassis components, and battery frame modules — meeting the dimensional and mechanical demands of global OEM supply chains.

  • Standard: TS16949
  • Applications: Body structures, chassis parts, NEV battery frames
  • Alloys: High-strength 6xxx series aluminium
  • Processes: Precision extrusion, CNC machining, heat treatment
  • Testing: Dimensional, tensile, and hardness inspection per batch
